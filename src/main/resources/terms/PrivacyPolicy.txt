At Finanger, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal and financial data.
————————————————————————————————————————————————————————
1. Information We Collect
We may collect the following types of information:
◆ Personal Information: Name, email, phone number (for account registration).
◆ Financial Data: Income, expenses, budgets, savings goals (entered by you).
◆ Device Information: IP address, browser type, app usage logs.
◆ Cookies & Tracking: Used to improve service experience.
2. How We Use Your Data
Your data is used for:
◆ Providing and improving Finanger’s financial tracking features.
◆ Generating reports and analytics (for your personal use only).
◆ Enhancing security and preventing fraud.
3. Data Sharing & Third Parties
We DO NOT sell your personal data. However, we may share data with:
◆ Service Providers (e.g., cloud storage, payment processors).
◆ Legal Authorities (only if required by law).
4. Data Security
We use industry-standard encryption to protect your financial data. However, no online system is 100% secure, so we encourage strong passwords.
5. Your RightsYou have the right to:
◆ Access your financial records.
◆ Edit or delete personal data (subject to legal restrictions).
◆ Opt-out of data tracking (some features may be affected).
6. Data Retention
Your data will be stored as long as you have an account. If you delete your account, data is permanently removed within 6 months, except where required by law.
7. Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this policy, and significant changes will be notified in the app.
